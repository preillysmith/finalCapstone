About Julius Restaurant

Julius was founded in 1992 by Mr. Reilly-Smith in the bustling centre of Winchester to provide residents with a cosy, authentic Italian eaterie on their doorsteps. Inspired by his time living in the rolling Tuscan hills, Reilly-Smith sought to recreate the culinary delights of the Italian countryside, and this mission continues today.

Visit our restaurant to indulge in the best Italian cuisine in Winchester or let us help you celebrate a special occasion with your friends and loved ones. Our varied and flexible menu suits all dietary requirements and tastes.
